Due to the loss of our Flight Instructor and Cherished Friend, Dick Johnson, JJ Aviation in no long offering local flight training. Also, due to the loss of our Piper Tomahawk we are no longer offering airplane rental.

Dick was one of the greatest men we have met in our lives. We were blessed to have had the honor of being his friends and business parters.

If your life was touched by Dick Johnson, please consider contacting Rod James in the General Engineering Department (406-496-4184) at Montana Tech of the University of Montana, and donating to the Memorial Scholarship being created in his name.

If you live in Montana and would like to learn to fly, might we recommend a few flight schools in our sister cities...

Mountain Airdance in Helena.

North Star Jet in Missoula.

Glacier Flight Training in Kalispell.

Northern Skies Aviation in Laural.

Summit Aviation in Bozeman.
